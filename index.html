About Me

Julia is currently the Communications Specialist for the Supply Chain Academy at Johnson & Johnson. She is responsible for developing graphics and communication materials to support the Supply Chain Academy, as well as designing frameworks that align with Johnson & Johnson’s broader communication strategies. Additionally, she tracks the effectiveness of the Academy’s tactical and strategic deliverables, so that she can provide quality services for senior leaders.


Prior to her employment with Johnson & Johnson, Julia worked in the financial sector, for Lincoln Financial Group and Nationwide Insurance Company. While a digital editor with Lincoln Financial Group, Julia managed digital content creation for LFG.com. Some of her responsibilities were developing the company’s digital editorial strategy, streamlining workflows and content direction, and creating effective executive presentations. For Nationwide Insurance Company, Julia was a market communications writer. In this role, she created, edited, and managed insurance documents held in third-party document repositories, as well as initiated improvements to customer support.


Julia also has a side business, Demestia Baking Company. Officially incorporated in 2014, the company sells bespoke classic American baked goods, such as layer cakes, cupcakes, brownies, pies, and cookies. Julia handles all the operations necessary to running the business, from baking the products to marketing them.


In addition to her employment, Julia is enrolled in a full stack web development course at the University of Pennsylvania. She finds the subject enthralling and knows it will be a great asset to her current position and beyond.


In her free time, Julia enjoys long-distance running, yoga, and photography.
