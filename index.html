About Me

Julia has been "force-feeding" friends and family since childhood. She would often bring treats to school and work in order to test out her ideas. After making a birthday cake out of a box for her high school boyfriend and best friend, she promised herself that was going to be the last time she did not make something from scratch, and has never looked back.
